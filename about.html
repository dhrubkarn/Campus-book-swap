Our Story

CampusBookSwap was founded in 2025 when a group of students realized that expensive textbooks often went unused after just one semester. What began as a small exchange initiative on campus soon evolved into a student-led platform dedicated to affordability and sustainability. Today, CampusBookSwap empowers thousands of students to save money, reduce waste, and support one another through community-driven book sharing..

Frequently Asked Questions

Is CampusBookSwap free to use?

+

Yes! CampusBookSwap is completely free for students. There are no hidden fees or commissions on book exchanges.

How do I ensure book quality?

+

We have a detailed condition rating system and user reviews. Always check the book condition and seller ratings before exchanging.

Can I rent books instead of buying?

+

Yes! Many students offer books for rent. Look for the "For Rent" tag when browsing books.

What if I have issues with a transaction?

+

Contact our support team immediately. We'll help resolve any disputes and maintain the integrity of our platform.
